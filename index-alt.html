The Gaming Experience

The makers of The Elder Scrolls, Bethesda, bring fantasy to real life in their games. Where you can do a plethora of things. From owning your own house and getting married to joining your favorite guilds/factions and slaying creatures of all shapes and sizes. This is why I enjoy their games more than most other video games. The open world has endless opportunity's, which allows me to let my mind run free, or I can even spend time strategizing to make a more powerful character and empire.

The possibilities are endless!
